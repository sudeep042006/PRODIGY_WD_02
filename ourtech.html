Step-by-Step: How It Works

  1. Patient wears smart wearable or biosensor
  2. Device collects real-time health data (e.g., heart rate, oxygen)
  3. Data is securely sent to our AI cloud engine
  4. AI analyzes and detects early disease patterns
  5. Doctors get real-time alerts with treatment suggestions

Use Case: Remote Village Healthcare

In remote areas with no nearby hospital, our portable health kits were deployed. Villagers wore smart patches. Within hours, early signs of heart conditions were identified, allowing remote doctors to intervene early. We prevented emergencies before they happened — **all through tech.**
